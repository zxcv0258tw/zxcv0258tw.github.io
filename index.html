About Me

I'm a graduate student in the Department of Electrical Engineering at National Tsing Hua University supervised by Prof. Min Sun in the Vision Science Lab.

My research interests are centered on computer vision, machine learning, especially in deep learning. My recent project focus on 3D model reconstruction and alignment from images and videos.
